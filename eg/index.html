Our Services

Individual Debt Collection

Individual debt collection is the process of recovering money owed by individuals who have not paid their debts on time. This includes personal loans, credit cards, unpaid bills, and other financial obligations.

Corporate Debt Collection

Corporate debt collection is the process of recovering money owed to a company from its clients or suppliers who have not paid their debts on time. This includes unpaid invoices, contract receivables, or loans. By providing flexible payment solutions such as debt installment plans.

Bank Collection

This is the process of recovering money owed to the bank from customers who have not paid their loans or financial obligations such as personal loans, mortgages, credit cards, or any other debts. This service includes several steps starting with communicating with debtors to remind them of payment, and providing flexible solutions such as debt rescheduling or installment of due amounts.

Government Sector Collection

Government sector debt collection relates to recovering money owed to government entities from individuals or companies, such as taxes, fees, fines, or other receivables. The collection process is carried out through multiple means, such as sending collection notices or providing flexible payment options.

Vehicle Repossession

Vehicle repossession service is a process carried out by banks, insurance companies, or financial institutions to repossess vehicles from their owners in case of non-payment of due installments or debts related to the vehicle. This service is usually performed after a period of warnings or alerts to debtors regarding settlement of due amounts.
